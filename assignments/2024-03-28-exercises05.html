  1. Use the free differential calculus to compute the Alexander polynomials of the trefoil and figure-8 knots.

  2. Prove that the genus of a knot is bounded below by half the breadth of the Alexander polynomial.

  3. Consider the map \(f : B_n \to B_n\) defined by \(f(e_i) = e_i^{-1}\). Prove that \(f\) is a homomorphism.

  4. Prove that \(B_n\) can be generated by just two elements, \(e_1\) and \(e_1e_2\ldots e_{n-1}\).

  5. Prove that the natural inclusion \(i : B_n \to B_{n+1}\) is injective.
