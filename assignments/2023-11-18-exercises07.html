  1. For a smooth manifold \(M\), define the Euler Characteristic, \(\chi(M) := \Sigma_{k=0}^{n} (-1)^k H^k(M)\). Prove that if \(M = U \cup V\), where \(U\) and \(V\) are open subsets of \(M\), than \(\chi(M) = \chi(U) + \chi(V) - \chi(U \cap V)\).

  2. Compute \(H_k(S^n)\) for each \(k \geq 0\) and \(n \geq 2\).

  3. Compute \(H_k(T)\) for each \(k \geq 0\) where \(T\) is the torus.
