  1. Consider a link \(L\) with \(n\) components.

    1. Prove that \(H_1(S^3 \setminus L) = \mathbb{Z}^n\)

    2. Prove that \(H_2(S^3 \setminus L) = \mathbb{Z}^{n-1}\)

  2. Let \(K\) denote the trefoil knot.

    1. Find a seifert surface for \(K\).

    2. Use that to compute \(H_1(X_2)\) where \(X_2\) denotes the 2-sheeted cover of \(S^3\setminus K\).

    3. Compute the Seifert matrix corresponding with the Seifert surface and use that to find \(H_(\tilde{X})\) as a \(\mathbb{Z}[t,t^{-1}]\), where \(\tilde{X}\) denotes the infinite cyclic cover.
